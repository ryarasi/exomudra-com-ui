// About Exomudra

Technology that adapts to humans.

Exomudra Technologies is a deep tech startup based in Chennai, India, building wireless smart gloves that enable natural hand gesture interaction with computers.

We believe the future of human-computer interaction isn't about better buttons — it's about removing them entirely. Our gloves capture precise finger movements using IMU and flex sensor fusion, translating them into digital commands for VR/AR, gaming, creative work, and assistive technology.

Founded in 2021 by Ragav Yarasi and incubated at iTNT Hub, we're on a mission to make gesture-based computing accessible and affordable — at 2–5x lower cost than competitors.
